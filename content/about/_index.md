## Hi, we're MesmerDesign 👋

MesmerDesign is a small, independent design studio based in the **Netherlands**. We make
**made-to-order gifts** — faith-filled sweatshirts, funny tees, personalized photo
keepsakes, teacher and mental-health mugs, and motivational wall art.

We started this blog for one simple reason: gift shopping is hard, and most "best gift"
lists are written by robots. Every guide here is **written by real people** who actually
care whether the gift lands. When we recommend a product, it's something we designed and
make ourselves — so you can read, decide, and order in one place.

### What we believe in

- **Meaning over clutter.** A gift should be used and loved, not stuffed in a drawer.
- **Made to order.** Less waste, more care.
- **Honest recommendations.** We tell you which pick is best for which person — including
  the budget option.
