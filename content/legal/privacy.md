## Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {{< current-year >}}_

This website (the MesmerDesign blog) is a static site that does not collect personal
information directly. We do not run user accounts, comments, or contact forms on this
site.

### Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics to understand which guides are useful.
Any such analytics are configured to avoid storing personally identifying information
where possible.

### Outbound links

Our guides link to product listings on **Etsy**. When you click through, Etsy's own
[Privacy Policy](https://www.etsy.com/legal/privacy/) applies. We add UTM tags to these
links (e.g. `utm_source=blog`) purely to understand which guides send shoppers to our
shop — they do not identify you personally.
