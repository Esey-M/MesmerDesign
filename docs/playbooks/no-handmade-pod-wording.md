Never describe MesmerDesign products as "handmade" (or "hand-made", "handcrafted"). The shop is **print-on-demand (POD)** — designs are printed/made to order by a supplier, not hand-crafted by the owner. Use **"made-to-order"**, **"printed to order"**, or **"print-on-demand"** instead.

**Why:** Calling POD products "handmade" is inaccurate and could mislead buyers / violate Etsy's handmade-claim rules. The owner caught and corrected this on 2026-06-19.

**How to apply:** When writing any blog post, product copy, disclosure, About text, etc., default to "made-to-order." The owner *designs* the products (that phrasing is fine: "we design"), but the items themselves are made to order, not handmade. See [[mesmerdesign-blog-project]].
